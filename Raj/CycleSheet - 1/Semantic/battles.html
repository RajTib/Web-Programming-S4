Battle of the Somme

Fought in 1916, the Battle of the Somme was one of the bloodiest battles in history. It marked the first large-scale use of tanks and resulted in over one million casualties.

Battle of Verdun

The Battle of Verdun was fought between France and Germany in 1916. It became a symbol of French resistance and attrition warfare, lasting nearly ten months.
