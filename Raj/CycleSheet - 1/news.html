AI Revolution in Education

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming the education sector. From automated grading to personalized learning paths, AI tools are helping students learn more efficiently.

However, experts believe that human guidance is still essential. AI should be used as a supporting tool rather than a complete replacement for teachers.
