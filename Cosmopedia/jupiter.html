Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system.

Distance from Sun 778.5 million km
Orbital Period 12 years
Moons 79
Atmosphere Hydrogen, Helium

Major Moons

  • Io
  • Europa
  • Ganymede
  • Callisto

Jupiter is known for the Great Red Spot, a huge storm system.
